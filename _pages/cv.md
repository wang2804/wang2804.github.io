Education
======
* Ph.D. in Building and Planning, National Taiwan University, Taiwan, 2000
* M.A. in Industrial Design, The Ohio State University, Columbus, OH, USA, 1991  
* B.S. in Industrial Design, Tatung Institute of Technology (now Tatung University), Taiwan, 1985  

Academic Appointments
======
* Acting Dean, College of Design, National Taipei University of Technology, 2026
* Associate Dean, College of Design, National Taipei University of Technology, 2026
* Professor, Department of Industrial Design, National Taipei University of Technology, 2023 - present
* Head, Department of Industrial Design, National Taipei University of Technology, 2009 - 2012
* Director, Undergraduate Program in Creative Design, National Taipei University of Technology, 2008 - 2009
* Former faculty member, Shih Chien University, 1998-2000
* Former faculty member, Tatung Institute of Technology, 1991-1995

Research Interests
======
* AI-enhanced industrial design
* Design education and research methodology
* Human–AI co-creation
* Biomimicry-inspired design
* Machine learning for product form and style analysis
* Design theory and epistemology
  
Work experience
======
* Design Consultant, Taipei City Government, Taiwan, 2011 - 2013
* Jury, Taipei Int’l Invention Show & Technomart, 2009 - 2014
* Member of the Board, Taipei Tech Developing Corp., 2012 - 2016
